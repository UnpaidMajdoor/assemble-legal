# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 8, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the Assemble team ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Assemble mobile application ("the App").

By using Assemble, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Assemble is a mobile application that helps people in Indian cities — starting with Bengaluru — discover groups, events, and other people for in-person meetups based on shared interests and location. The App is currently operated by an individual founder team based in India ("the Assemble team").

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: **hello@assembleclub.com**

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Assemble:

### 2.1 Information you provide directly

- **Phone number** — used to create and authenticate your account via Firebase Phone Authentication (one-time password / OTP).
- **First name and last name** — displayed on your profile and visible to other users in groups, events, and chats.
- **Date of birth** — collected at signup to verify that you are 18 years or older. We do not display your full date of birth to other users; we may show your age or age range.
- **Gender** — collected to support safety features (such as women-only group options) and visibility settings.
- **Profile photo and other photos** — images you upload for your profile, groups, and events.
- **Interests** — categories you select to help us suggest relevant groups and events to you.
- **Content you create** — messages in group chats, events you create, group descriptions, and any other content you post inside the App.
- **Reports and blocks** — when you report or block another user, we record this for moderation and safety purposes.

### 2.2 Information we collect automatically

- **Location data (with your permission)** — when you grant location permission, we collect your approximate or precise GPS location to show you nearby groups, events, and people, and to display distances. You can turn this permission off at any time in your device settings; some features will not work without it.
- **Device information** — operating system, OS version, device model, app version, language, and time zone. This helps us debug issues and serve content correctly.
- **Usage and analytics data** — which screens you open, which buttons you tap, time spent in the App, and similar interaction data. We use this to understand how the App is used and to improve it (see Section 4).
- **Crash and error data** — when the App crashes or hits an error, we capture technical details (stack trace, device state, OS version) so we can fix bugs.

### 2.3 Information we do NOT collect

- We do **not** collect your email address.
- We do **not** read your phonebook or contacts.
- We do **not** access your microphone unless you record video content within the App.
- We do **not** collect or store any payment information. The App is free to use.
- We do **not** collect Aadhaar, PAN, or any government-issued identity numbers.

If we change what we collect in the future (for example, adding email signup or contacts-based invites), we will update this policy and notify users before the change takes effect.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Authenticate your identity and operate your account
- Match you with relevant groups, events, and people based on your location and interests
- Display your profile, photos, and content to other users in the contexts you choose (groups you join, events you create or attend)
- Send you notifications about activity in your groups, events you might like, and weekly nudges (you can disable these in settings)
- Investigate and act on reports of harassment, abuse, fake accounts, or violations of our Terms of Service
- Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, security incidents, and technical issues
- Improve the App's features, design, and performance
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

We do **not** use your data to show you advertisements. Assemble currently has no advertising.

---

## 4. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use third-party services to understand App usage and to capture errors:

### Amplitude (Analytics)

We use Amplitude to track in-app events such as screen views, taps, signups, group joins, and event RSVPs. Amplitude receives anonymized usage data tied to a user identifier (not your phone number directly). This helps us understand which features work well and which need improvement.

Amplitude's privacy policy: https://amplitude.com/privacy

### Sentry (Crash Reporting)

We use Sentry to capture errors, crashes, and exceptions in the App. When the App crashes, Sentry receives technical details: the type of error, the file and line where it occurred, the device model, OS version, and what you were doing in the App immediately before the crash. We use this only to debug and fix issues.

Sentry's privacy policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

We do not sell or share your data with these services for advertising or marketing purposes. They are used only for product improvement and reliability.

## 5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in these specific situations:

### 5.1 With other Assemble users

- Your name, profile photo, gender, age, and selected interests are visible to other users in groups you join, events you attend, and contexts you actively participate in.
- Your messages in group chats are visible to other members of those groups.
- Your approximate distance from other users may be shown (e.g., "4 km away"), but never your exact GPS coordinates.

### 5.2 With service providers

We use trusted third-party providers to operate the App. These providers receive only the data they need to perform their function:

- **Firebase (Google)** — phone authentication and OTP delivery
- **Supabase** — database and backend storage
- **Amplitude** — analytics
- **Sentry** — crash reporting
- **Google Maps Platform** — location and map services

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy and data handling commitments.

### 5.3 For legal reasons

We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law, court order, or other valid legal process — for example, in response to a request from Indian law enforcement under the Information Technology Act, 2000 or the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

### 5.4 In case of business transfer

If the Assemble team is acquired, merges with another entity, or transfers ownership of the App, your data may be transferred to the new owner. We will notify you in advance and you will have the option to delete your account before the transfer takes effect.

---

## 6. How We Store and Protect Your Data

- Your data is stored on servers operated by our backend providers (currently Supabase and Firebase). These services use industry-standard security practices including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and encryption at rest.
- Database access is restricted using row-level security: users can only read and write their own data and the data of groups they are members of. We do not have a habit of routinely accessing user data; access is limited to debugging, support requests you initiate, and moderation in response to reports.
- We do not guarantee absolute security. No system is completely safe from breaches. If a breach occurs that affects you, we will notify you as required by law.

---

## 7. How Long We Keep Your Data

- **Account data** is kept as long as your account is active.
- **If you delete your account**, we delete your profile, photos, and personal information within 30 days. Some content may remain in backups for up to 90 days, after which it is permanently deleted.
- **Messages in group chats** may continue to appear in those groups under a "Deleted user" label after you leave or delete your account, because the messages are part of the group's shared history. We do not display your name or photo with these messages after deletion.
- **Reports, blocks, and moderation records** may be retained beyond account deletion for safety and legal purposes.
- **Anonymized analytics data** may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.

## 8. Your Rights

Under Indian law (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023) and good-practice standards, you have the right to:

- **Access** your data — see what we have collected about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data — update your profile, photos, interests, and other fields
- **Delete** your data and account — through the App's settings, or by emailing us at hello@assembleclub.com
- **Withdraw consent** for specific data uses — for example, by disabling location permission or push notifications in your device settings
- **Object to processing** of your data for purposes you disagree with
- **Receive a copy** of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at **hello@assembleclub.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

## 9. Children Under 18

**Assemble is for adults aged 18 and older.** We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

At signup, we ask for your date of birth to verify your age. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will delete their account and any associated data.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has created an account on Assemble, please contact us at **hello@assembleclub.com** and we will remove the account.

This policy is consistent with our obligations under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012 (POCSO) and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

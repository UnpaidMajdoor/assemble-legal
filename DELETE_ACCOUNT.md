# How to Delete Your Assemble Account

**Last updated: May 9, 2026**

This page explains how to permanently delete your Assemble account and what happens to your data when you do.

## How to delete your account

You can delete your Assemble account directly within the app. To do this:

1. Open the Assemble app on your phone.
2. Sign in with your phone number.
3. Tap the **Profile** tab in the bottom navigation bar.
4. Scroll down and tap **Delete Account**.
5. Confirm the deletion when prompted. You'll be asked to select a reason and re-authenticate with an OTP.
6. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers within 24 hours:

- Your profile (name, phone number, date of birth, city, gender, interests, profile photo)
- Your authentication record (Firebase Auth)
- Your group memberships and any groups you created (if you are the only member)
- Your event RSVPs
- Your messages in group chats
- Your reports of other users
- Your blocks of other users
- Your support tickets and feedback submissions
- All analytics data tied to your user ID (Amplitude)
- All crash reports tied to your user ID (Sentry)

## What is retained (and for how long)

Some data is retained for limited operational, legal, or security reasons:

- **Aggregate usage statistics** that contain no personally identifiable information (e.g., daily active user counts) — retained indefinitely for product improvement.
- **Records related to fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service** — retained for up to 90 days for security purposes, then permanently deleted.
- **Backups** — your data may persist in encrypted database backups for up to 30 days, after which it is permanently overwritten.

After 90 days, no personally identifiable information about your account remains on our servers.

## Account deletion outside the app

If you cannot access the app to delete your account (for example, you've lost access to your phone number), you can request deletion by emailing us at:

**hello@assembleclub.com**

Please email us from the email address associated with your account if possible, or include your phone number in the subject line so we can verify your identity. We will process your request within 7 business days.

## Questions

If you have questions about account deletion or data retention, contact us at **hello@assembleclub.com**.
